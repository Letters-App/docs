## View Your Invoices

To view and download your invoices:

1. Go to the **Settings** page.
2. Click the **Billing** tab.
3. Download the invoice by clicking on the manage subscription button in the top right.

## Manage Your Payment Method

Update your payment method:

1. Go to the **Settings** page.
2. Click the **Billing** tab.
3. Add or update your payment method by clicking the manage subscription button in the top right/
