If you're on an active plan, you'll be able to manage your subscription & payment details.

## View Your Invoices & Payment Method

To view and download your invoices, and update your payment method:

1. Go to the **Settings** page.

2. Click the **Billing** tab.

3. Download the invoice by clicking **Manage Subscription** in the top right.
