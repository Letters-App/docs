---
title: 'Your First Transcription'
description: 'How to create your first transcription by recording or uploading audio.'
icon: 'spinner'
---

<Steps>

  <Step title="Transcription Setup">
  To create your first transcription, click **New Transcription** in the transcribe pane of the homepage, and give it a name.

<Note>Consultation mode allows you to detect multiple speakers.</Note>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Record and Upload Audio">
  Record a new transcription by clicking **Start** in the record menu of the record audio pane. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/recordtranscript.jpg" />
</Frame>

Alternatively, upload an audio file from the upload menu.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/upload audio.jpg" />
</Frame>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Transcription Generated">
  Upon completion of a recording, a transcript will be generated in the transcript pane, which offers various editing and organisation options.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Name Speakers" icon="microphone">
    In consultation transcription format, speakers are labelled by number. You can assign each speaker a unique name here.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/namespeaker.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Copy" icon="copy">
    Copy the transcription text for use in other applications.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Edit" icon="pencil">
    Manually edit the generated transcription text.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Action Menu" icon="bolt">
    Use the transcription text with the AI Assistant to ask questions or generate letters, or attach it to a patient letter.

     <Frame>
      <img src="/images/save.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Intelligence">
  Intelligence allows you to customise how transcription intelligence works.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manageintelligence.jpg" />
  </Frame>

  <AccordionGroup>

    <Accordion title="Custom Instructions" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Customise output with your own instructions, such as language style or summary format.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Type" icon="list">
    Choose the style of transcription, such as:
    - **Summary**: A concise overview focusing on key points.
    - **SOAP Notes**: Uses the standard medical format (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan).
    - **Patient-Friendly**: Simplifies language for patient understanding.
    - **Detailed Report**: Provides comprehensive detail with minimal summarisation.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Format" icon="paragraph">
    Select how the transcription is structured:
    - **Freestyle**: No predefined structure for flexible output.
    - **Headings & Bullet Points**: Organised for easy scanning.
    - **Headings & Paragraphs**: Structured for detailed documentation.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Additional Instructions" icon="comment">
    Provide further instructions for the AI to generate the transcription.
    </Accordion>

  </AccordionGroup>

  </Step>

</Steps>
