---
title: 'Your First Transcription'
description: 'How to create your first transcription by recording or uploading audio.'
icon: 'spinner'
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Transcription Setup">
    To create your first transcription, click **New Transcription** in the transcribe pane on the homepage. You will be prompted to enter the Patient Name to begin.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Consultation Mode">
    You can choose to use consultation mode. This mode allows Letters to detect multiple speakers and name them accordingly in the transcription output.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Record and Upload Audio">
    Record a new transcription by clicking **Start** in the record menu of the audio pane.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/recordtranscript.jpg" />
    </Frame>

    Alternatively, upload an audio file from the upload menu.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/upload audio.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Your Notes">
    You can also input your own notes to be used in the transcription for things you have not said out aloud. 

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/yournotes.jpg" />
    </Frame>

  <Step title="Transcription Generated">
    Upon completion of a recording, a transcript will be generated in the transcript pane, which offers various editing and organisation options.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Detect and Name Speakers" icon="microphone">
        If using consultation mode, you can detect and name the speakers by clicking manage speakers.

        <Frame>
          <img src="/images/namespeaker.jpg" />
        </Frame>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Edit" icon="pencil">
        Manually edit the generated transcription text.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Write" Letter" icon=letter">
        Start a new Letter and automaticlly add the transcription as a source.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Action Menu" icon="bolt">
        Copy the transcript into a conversation with Assistant, attach it as a source to a Letter, or copy it to use elsewhere.

        <Frame>
          <img src="/images/save.jpg" />
        </Frame>
      </Accordion>

      
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Intelligence">
    Intelligence allows you to customise how transcription intelligence works. You can enable intelligence in the Transcription Output Pane.&#x20;

    You will be given the option to choose the type of Intelligence:&#x20;

    * **Summary**: A concise overview focusing on key points.

    * **Patient-Friendly**: Simplifies language for patient understanding.

    * **SOAP Notes**: Uses the standard medical format (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan).

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/viewintelligence.jpg)
    </Frame>

    You can also customise your Intelligence by Adjusting the format or adding Additional instructions to provide further instructions for the AI to generate the transcription.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/manageintelligence.jpg)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
