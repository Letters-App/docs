## Creating a Transcription
To create your first transcription, **"Click"** New Transcription in the transcribe pane of the homepage, and give it a name. Each transcription may either be <Tooltip tip="Transcription of a Single Speaker">Dictation</Tooltip> or a <Tooltip tip="Transcription of a Multiple Speakers">Consultation</Tooltip>.

  <Step title="Transcription Generated">
  Upon completion of a recording, a transcript will be generated in transcript pane. The transcript pane provides various editing and organisation options.
  
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Name Speakers" icon="microphone">
    In consulation transcription format, each speaker is labelled by number. Each speaker can be given a unique name in this menu.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Copy" icon="copy">
    Copy the transcription text for uses in other applications.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Edit" icon="pencil">
    Manually edit the generated transcription text.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Action Menu" icon="bolt">
    Copy the transcription text to an <Tooltip tip="Ask AI questions regarding your letter">AI Chat</Tooltip>, or <Tooltip tip="Allows you to link a patient letter and transcript file">attach to a letter</Tooltip>.  
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/blank.png" />
</Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Intelligence">
     Intelligence allow you customise how transcription intelligence works.

  <AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Custom Instructions" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Customise intelligence output by entering your own instructions such as language style, or summary format.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Type" icon="list">
    Customise the style of transcription including <Tooltip tip="Provides a concise overview of the transcription, focusing on key points and main takeaways.">Summary</Tooltip>, <Tooltip tip="Transcribes the dictation using the SOAP format (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan), a standard method for documenting clinical notes.">SOAP Notes</Tooltip>, <Tooltip tip="Simplifies the dictation to make it easily understandable for patients by removing medical jargon and technical terms,">Patient-Friendly</Tooltip> and <Tooltip tip="Provides a thorough and in-depth transcription of the dictation, including all details and observations with minimal summarisation.">Detailed Report</Tooltip>.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Format" icon="paragraph">
    Customise the structure of transcription including <Tooltip tip="Generate without a predefined structure, allowing full flexibility in how the content is presented.">Freestyle</Tooltip>, <Tooltip tip="Organises the transcription into sections with headings and bullet points, making it easy to scan and read quickly.">Headings & Bullet Points</Tooltip> and <Tooltip tip="Organises the transcription into sections with headings and paragraphs, providing a more detailed and structured output.">Headings & Paragraphs</Tooltip>.

    <Accordion title="Additional Instructions" icon="comment">
