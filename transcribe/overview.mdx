## Transcribe

- Transcribes both **dictation style** and **consultations** (with speaker detection).
- Supports live and pre-recorded audio in virtually any format.
- Delivers fast, highly accurate transcriptions without needing voice training.
- Automatically applies grammar and follows custom instructions, like spelling out unknown words.
- **Customisable intelligence** converts transcriptions into formats such as patient summaries, SOAP notes, and more.
