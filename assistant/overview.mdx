The **Assistant** is your AI-powered tool, designed specifically for medical professionals. It helps with drafting practice materials, converting consultation notes into letters, and much more through intuitive text generation and manipulation.

## Key Features

<Card title="Attachments" icon="paperclip">
  Easily attach files within the Assistant. Upload patient documents, test results, or reference materials, and the Assistant will use them to generate or refine content.

<Card title="Dictation" icon="microphone">

<Card title="Shortcuts" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
