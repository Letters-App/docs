## Connecting a Microphone for Letters

A high-quality microphone is essential for accurate transcription in Letters. Your deviceâ€™s built-in microphone can be used for dictating in Letters, but when transcribing consultations, it's important to pay attention to its placement. Ensuring the microphone is in range of all speakers will help Letters detect and isolate individuals in your transcription.&#x20;

### How to Connect:

1. Connect your microphone to your device.

2. Open Google Chrome.

3. Open [Letters](http://my.letters.app).

4. When prompted, allow Chrome to use your microphone.

### Troubleshooting:

* Ensure the microphone is securely plugged in.

* Adjust Google Chrome settings to allow microphone access.

* Test your microphone on other apps or restart your system.
