## Privacy and Security Overview

Absolute privacy and security is Company A's top priority, with all sensitive information processed and stored within Australia in compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

### Key Security Measures:

1. **Real-time Transcription**: No audio is stored permanently, and transcription happens immediately on servers in Sydney.
2. **LLM Processing**: Data is processed locally without being used for training or external access.
3. **Encrypted Documents**: Files are encrypted and saved only temporarily, with flexible retention settings.

For further details, visit [this page](https://help.lyrebirdhealth.com/en/articles/8738063-privacy-and-security-overview).

## Medicolegal Compliance:

Company A is designed with medicolegal compliance in mind, ensuring consent is obtained before consultations. Over 99.5% of patients consent on their first consult.
