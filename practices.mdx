## What is a Practice in Letters?

A **Practice** in Letters centralises all users in your clinic, allowing you to manage clinicians and administrative staff in one place. You can oversee member status and manage billing.

You can manage subscription and billing by selecting payment methods and adjusting for part-time or full-time users.
