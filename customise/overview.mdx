<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Letters" icon="envelope">
    - **Letter Types**: Create and manage your personalised Letter Types.
    - **Saved Edits**: Edits saved & applied when writing letters.
    - **Letter Detail Fields**: Add optional fields for age, date, pronoun & more to Letter Input.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transcribe" icon="microphone">
    - **Dictionary**: Add frequently misidentified words to improve accuracy.
    - **Custom Intelligence Type**: Give instructions for your own Intelligence Type.
    - **Skip Transcript**: Process audio directly into Intelligence for faster processing.
    - **Remove Small Talk**: Filter out casual, non-medical conversation from the transcript.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Assistant" icon="message">
    - **Shortcuts**: Create shortcuts for frequently used requests.
    - **Memory**: Adjust memory for future conversations.
    - **Voice**: Change the voice when using spoken messages.
