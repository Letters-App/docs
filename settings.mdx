## Profile Settings
- **Name & Email**: Update your first name, last name, and email.
- **Field of Practice**: Choose your area of expertise to tailor how Letters generates documents.

## Preferences
- **Document Retention**: Set how long documents are saved (from 24 hours to 6 months).
- **Audio Recordings**: Enable or disable storage of dictation audio.
- **Stream Dictation**: See real-time transcription during dictation.

## Security
- Enable **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** for enhanced account protection.
