## Welcome to Letters

Letters is a user-first letter writing tool tailored for medical professionals. Letters features industry-leading transcription accuracy, and intelligently designed AI-powered tools to minimise your paperwork.

### Key Features

Letters focuses on three core pages: **Letters**, **Transcribe**, and **Assistant**.

* **Letters**: Create medical letters than sound like you, and are structured like yours from a variety of sources in a instant.&#x20;

* **Transcribe**: Dictate post-consult thoughts, listen to multiple speakers, and don't miss any crucial details.

* **Assistant**: Automate daunting tasks and chat with a medical, secure and private AI assistant..

* **My Customisations**: Delve deeper and tailor every aspect of ours features to your liking.
