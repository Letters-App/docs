The **Assistant** is your AI-powered tool, designed specifically for medical professionals. It helps with drafting practice materials, converting consultation notes into letters, and much more through intuitive text generation and manipulation.

### Key Features

#### Attachments
Easily attach files within the Assistant. Upload patient documents, test results, or reference materials, and the Assistant will use them to generate or refine content as needed.

#### Dictation
Dictate directly into the Assistant for fast transcription. Speak consultation notes or letters, and the Assistant will instantly convert your dictation into structured documents, including SOAP notes or complete letters.

  <img src="/images/chatrecord.jpg" />

### Additional Capabilities
- Generate custom letters and summaries.
- Automate responses or documents based on attached files or dictation.
