## How to Transfer Notes

Letters is designed for easy and seamless transfer of your notes into any medical record system.

### Step-by-Step Guide:
1. Click the **"Copy"** button at the bottom right of the note or document.
2. Paste the copied content into your medical record (Right-click → Paste or Command ⌘ + V).
