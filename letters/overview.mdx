title: 'Introduction to Letters'
description: 'AI-powered letter writing tailored for medical professionals.'
---

Includes core details, text, transcriptions, and typed/scanned PDFs to generate your letter.
Letters are based on customisable templates. Templates can be created or adjusted by users and mimic their style when a few examples are submitted.
Features include version history, the ability to request changes, and other customisations.
