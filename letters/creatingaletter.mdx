## Creating a New Letter
To create your first letter, **"Click"** new letter in the letters pane of the homepage, and give it a name. The letters creation page is dividing into two mian sections, **Inputs** and **Outputs**.

### Letter Inputs
Letter inputs control the information the LLM uses to create your custom letter.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Report Template">
  <Note>All Letters Must Include a <Tooltip tip="Al will learn these templates, writing reports that are structured to sound like you.">Report Template</Tooltip></Note>
    Create your first report template by clicking the **"Create Template"** button.

    Give the template a **Name**, **Description**, and upload a minimum of **3 Letters** to train the AI on your writing style. Once processed, the template can be selected in the input dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Patient Details">
    Input your pateint details including **Pateint Name**, **Age**, **Gender**, **Consulation Date** and **Family Details**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notes">
    These are instructions or content that only pertain to the third step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sources">
    These are instructions or content that only pertain to the third step.
  </Step>
</Steps>
