## Creating a New Letter

To create your first letter, Click **New Letter** in the Letters pane of the homepage, and give it a name. The letters creation page is divided into two main sections, <Tooltip tip="The Letter setup pane controls the information that we use to create your custom letter.">**Setup** </Tooltip>and <Tooltip tip="This is where Letters generates your custom letter. There are options to customsie the output and export it.">**Output**.</Tooltip>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Letter Types">
    <Note>All letters must include a<Tooltip tip="Letter Types allow the AI to learn your writing style and write letters that are structured to sound like you."> Letter Type</Tooltip></Note>
    Create your first Letter Type by clicking the **Create Letter Type** button.

    Give the type a **Name**, **Description**, and upload a minimum of **3 Sample Letters** in the upload window. Samples letters help to train the AI on your writing style, uploading more letters may improve the letter output. Once processed, the type can be selected in the setup menu dropdown.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/createtemplate1.jpg" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/createtemplate2.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient Details">
    Input your patient details including **Name**, **Age**, **Pronoun** and **Consultation Date**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sources">
    <Note>All Letters must include at least one source.</Note>

    Sources include <Tooltip tip="Ability to dictate direclty into the letter">quick dications</Tooltip>, <Tooltip tip="Text allows you to provide quick notes to the AI on details on your patient consulations or instructions on how to generate a letter">text</Tooltip>, <Tooltip tip="Files include prewritten patient information such as handwritten notes and digital PDFs">File Upload</Tooltip> or <Tooltip tip="Patient information transcribed from previous audio recordings">transcriptions</Tooltip>.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Quick Dictate" icon="microphone">
        Dictate directly into letters to provide details on the consultation or further notes for the AI to use in generating the Letter.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Text" icon="keyboard">
        Text provides the AI details on your patient consulation.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="File Upload" icon="paperclip">
        Documents can be uploaded directly by clicking the **Add** button, and adding the relevant files.

        <Frame>
          <img src="/images/letterattach.jpg" />
        </Frame>

        <Note>Smart document analysis accounts for visual complexity if the PDF was scanned, contains handwriting, or is visually complex.</Note>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Transcriptions" icon="microphone">
        Transcriptions can be uploaded by clicking the **Add** button. Previously generated transcriptions can be selected, or a new transcription generated by clicking **New Transcription**.

        <Frame>
          <img src="/images/lettertranscription.jpg" />
        </Frame>
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Writing Your Letter">
    Your letter can be written by clicking **Write Letter**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Letter Outputs">
    Letters generates your custom letter. Options to customise your letter are shown below.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Version History" icon="clock">
        Version history stores all letter modifications by time and date. A previous version can be restored at any time by clicking **Restore**.

        <Frame>
          <img src="/images/lettershistory.jpg" />
        </Frame>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Requesting Changes" icon="pencil">
        Clicking **Request Changes** allows the AI to rewrite your letter based on your specific feedback. The AI can remember your feedback for future letters by selecting <Tooltip tip="Your requested change will be used when writing future letters">Save for Future</Tooltip>.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Editing and Exporting Your Letter" icon="paper-plane">
        The **Edit** button allows your to edit the letter output text manually. Once complate, all text can be copied for use in other software by clicking **Copy**.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>

  </Step>
</Steps>
