## Creating a New Letter
To create your first letter, **"Click"** new letter in the letters pane of the homepage, and give it a name. The letters creation page is dividing into two main sections, <Tooltip tip="Letter inputs control the information that we use to create your custom letter.">**Input**</Tooltip> and <Tooltip tip="This is where letters generates your custom letter. There are options to customsie the output and export it.">**Output**.</Tooltip>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Report Template">
  <Note>All Letters Must Include a <Tooltip tip="Report templates allow the AI to learn your writing style and write reports that are structured to sound like you.">Report Template</Tooltip></Note>
    Create your first report template by clicking the **Create Template** button.

    Give the template a **Name**, **Description**, and upload a minimum of **3 Sample Letters** in the upload window. Samples letters help to train the AI on your writing style, uploading more letters may improve letter outputs. Once processed, the template can be selected in the input dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Patient Details">
    Input your patient details including **Pateint Name**, **Age**, **Pronoun**, **Consultation Date** and **Family Details**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notes">
    <Note>All Letters Must Include Either Notes or Sources</Note>

    Notes provide the AI details on your patient consulation. They may be manually written in the dialog box, or recorded by clicking the **Microphone** icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sources">
    Sources include <Tooltip tip="Documents include prewritten patient information such as handwritten notes and digital PDFs">documents</Tooltip> and <Tooltip tip="Patient information transcribed from previous audio recordings">transcriptions</Tooltip>. Documents can be uploaded directly by clicking the **Add** button, and adding the relevant files.

    <Note><Tooltip tip="Accounts for visual complexity if the PDF was scanned, contains handwriting, or is visually complex.">Smart Document Analysis</Tooltip> Must Be Selected Prior to Upload</Note>

    Transcriptions can be uploaded by clicking the **Add** button. Previously generated transcriptions can be selected, or a new transcription generated by clicking **New Transcription**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Writing Your Letter">
  Your letter can be written by clicking **Write Letter**.
  </Step>

<Step title="Letter Outputs">
  Letters generates your custom letter. Options to customise your letter are shown below.
  <AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Version History" icon="clock">
    Version history stores all letter modifications by time and date. A previous version can be restored at any time by clicking **Restore**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Requesting Changes" icon="pencil">
    Clicking **Request Changes** allows the AI to rewrite your letter based on your specific feedback. The AI can remember your feedback for future letters by selecting <Tooltip tip="Your requested change will be used when writing future letters">Save for Future</Tooltip>.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Editing and Exporting Your Letter" icon="paper-plane">
    The **Edit** button allows your to edit the letter output text manually. Once complate, all text can be copied for use in other software by clicking **Copy**. 
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>
